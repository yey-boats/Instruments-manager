Flash new device (USB)

Plug a fresh Sunton ESP32-4848S040 into this computer over USB, then do two things in order: (1) flash the firmware, then (2) provision it onto the boat WiFi. Both steps talk to the device directly from your browser over USB serial.

USB flashing needs a secure context. Open this page via http://localhost (e.g. the SSH tunnel) or HTTPS, on the computer the device is plugged into. Browsers only expose USB serial (navigator.serial) to secure origins, so the buttons below are hidden until then.

Step 1. Flash firmware

Pick the firmware build, then click Connect and follow the prompts to erase & write.

WebSerial is not supported in this browser. Use Chrome or Edge over HTTPS / localhost. Serial access was not granted.

Step 2. Provision over serial

After the device finishes flashing and reboots, click below. The manager sends the boat WiFi credentials over USB serial. The device number and OTA password are applied later by the server once the device registers, so they are not sent here.
